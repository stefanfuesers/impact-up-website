Privacy Notice

We take the protection of your data seriously and comply with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the German Federal Data Protection Act (BDSG). This page is intentionally lean – we use no trackers, no analytics cookies and no advertising scripts.

2. Hosting and server log files

This site is hosted by IONOS SE, Elgendorfer Straße 57, 56410 Montabaur, Germany. When the page is accessed, technically necessary data is transmitted to the server and stored in so-called log files: IP address (anonymised or shortened), date and time, requested page, referrer, browser type and operating system. This data serves solely the stable operation of the website (Art. 6 (1) lit. f GDPR – legitimate interest) and is automatically deleted after a short retention period. A data processing agreement under Art. 28 GDPR is in place with IONOS.

3. Contact and first-conversation form

When you contact us via the first-conversation form or by e-mail, we process the transmitted data (e-mail address, optional website, request, note) solely to respond to your enquiry (Art. 6 (1) lit. b and f GDPR).

At present, the form opens your own e-mail client (mailto delivery) – your data therefore goes directly from you to us, without intermediate storage on a third-party server. A direct form submission via a processor is in preparation; this section will be updated accordingly before the switch.

We do not pass your data on to third parties. Once your enquiry is closed and no statutory retention obligations stand against it, we delete the data.

4. Fonts (Google Fonts)

This site loads fonts ("Cormorant Garamond" and "Inter") from Google Fonts. When the page is accessed, a connection is established to servers of Google (Google Ireland Limited, Gordon House, Barrow Street, Dublin 4, Ireland), in the course of which your IP address is transmitted. Legal basis is our legitimate interest in a consistent, fast presentation (Art. 6 (1) lit. f GDPR). Self-hosting of the fonts is planned for a later version.

5. External links

This site links to external websites (e.g. Agentur für Zukunftsgestaltung, Co-Creators Circle, UBIE). We have no influence on their privacy practices; please inform yourself directly there.

6. Your rights

You have the right at any time to access (Art. 15 GDPR), rectification (Art. 16), erasure (Art. 17), restriction of processing (Art. 18), data portability (Art. 20) and objection (Art. 21). A short e-mail to info@impact-up.org is sufficient.

You may also lodge a complaint with a data protection supervisory authority – for Hamburg, this is the Hamburg Commissioner for Data Protection and Freedom of Information.

7. Currency

This notice applies as of May 2026. If anything changes about the website or the services in use, we update it accordingly.

Impulses

Impulses are what we share from our work with the world: thoughts, methods, observations – sometimes just a question that won't let us go. From a project, from the literature, from a conversation that set something in motion. We share them because good impact grows when you make it visible and pass it on – and because the good things in this world multiply when many people keep thinking them further. Look around, take what speaks to you, and follow the thought wherever it leads.

Impact Up Constructs

Constructs are the theoretical foundations of Impact Up – the thinking tools our work has grown from. We call them constructs because we built them to see and discuss impact relationships. Every description of the world is a construction – and good constructions make working together possible: they give everyone involved a language for what is at work between them. Three of them carry our work.

01

Impact Up Philosophy

Why we work the way we work – and what drives us. An impact never stands alone: from this observation an institute has grown that stays close to people and projects while keeping the field in view where impacts interlock.

→ To the Philosophy

 02

Systemic Impact Orientation

From individual impact to effect across the whole field – the term we are shaping. Systemic impact orientation has two directions: designing impact orientation with a systemic stance – and orienting towards impact that changes and carries the system itself.

→ To Systemic Impact Orientation

 03

Systems Change

An approach to social change with a systemic claim – established in the English-speaking field; we bring it into the German-speaking context. For us, one of several approaches that systemic impact orientation draws on.

→ To Systems Change
